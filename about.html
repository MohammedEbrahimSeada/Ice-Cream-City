Our Story

Founded by a team of ice cream lovers, IceCream City began as a tiny scoop shop with a big dream, to spread joy one scoop at a time. We believe in real ingredients, bold flavors, and ice cream for all seasons. We believe that one scoop of ice cream can completely change your mood.

“You can’t buy happiness, but you can buy ice cream. And that’s kind of the same thing.” 😊🍨

Fun Ice Cream Facts

  • Ice cream dates back to 200 BC in China!
  • The U.S. consumes the most ice cream per capita in the world.
  • Vanilla is still the most popular flavor worldwide.
  • There are over 1,000 different flavors of ice cream globally.
  • Ice cream can be made from a variety of bases, including dairy, coconut milk, and even avocado!
  • National Ice Cream Day is celebrated on the third Sunday of July in the U.S.
  • In Japan, you can find unique flavors like wasabi and squid ink!
